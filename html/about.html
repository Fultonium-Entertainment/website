Who we are?

ISO Crazy Studios (for short, ISO Studios) is a company of young, ambitious people who specialize in animation and various aspects of music and art. We work together on the Discord platform to come up with novel ideas and create projects that are meant to resonate with individuals who struggle with mental health and/or identify as neurodivergent.
