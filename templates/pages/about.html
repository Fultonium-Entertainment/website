Who we are?

Fultonic Entertainment (formerly, ISO Studios) is a company of young, ambitious people who specialize in animation and various aspects of music and art. We work together on the Discord platform to come up with novel ideas and create projects that are meant to resonate with individuals who struggle with mental health and/or identify as neurodivergent.

What we do?

We produce shows, anime, and high quality animations. We aim to give jobs to anyone who can recieve it.

Company Values

  • Progress comes first.
  • Authenticity is invaluable.
  • Incentivize teamwork with profit.
  • Hard work & dedication
  • Efficiency & time management
  • Professional & emotional support for everyone
